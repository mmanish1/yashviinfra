Metal False Ceiling Systems in Ahmedabad & Gujarat

Yashvi Infra Co is a trusted metal false ceiling contractor specializing in aluminum and galvanized iron (GI) ceiling systems. We supply and install premium ceiling solutions across Ahmedabad, Surat, Vadodara, and all of Gujarat.

Our metal false ceiling systems feature powder-coated or color-coated finishes, offering durability, aesthetics, and superior performance. We provide multiple design options including linear panels, baffle ceilings, lay-in tiles, clip-in systems, and open cell configurations—each tailored to your specific project requirements and budget.

Expert Aluminum & GI Panel Solutions

Lightweight, corrosion-resistant aluminum panels and economical, durable GI options for any space.

Multiple Ceiling Styles

Linear 84C/84R, Linear 150F, U-shaped baffles, lay-in tiles, clip-in systems, and open cell designs.

Professional Installation

Complete project management from design consultation to final installation with quality assurance.
