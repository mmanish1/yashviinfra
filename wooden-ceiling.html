Ceiling Profile

Which Metal Ceiling Profile Works Best for Wooden Finish?

Wooden grain finish is available on two panel profiles — 84C and 184C. Here's how to choose the right width for your space.

84C

Linear 84C — 84mm Panel Width

Narrower panels create a finer, more detailed linear wood look — more grain lines visible, giving the appearance of individual timber planks laid side by side.

Best for: Bedrooms, living rooms, small offices, restaurant booths, corridors — anywhere a finer, more natural plank-like look is preferred.

184C

Linear 184C — 184mm Panel Width ⭐ Recommended

Wider panels create a bolder, more dramatic wooden ceiling look — fewer visible joints, giving the appearance of wide timber board planks in a premium installation.

Best for: Hotel lobbies, showrooms, large living rooms, open-plan offices — anywhere a bold, premium wood ceiling statement is required.
