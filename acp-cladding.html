What Is It?

What is ACP Cladding?

ACP (Aluminium Composite Panel) cladding is a flat panel consisting of two thin aluminium sheets bonded to a non-aluminium core. It is widely used as exterior facade cladding for buildings due to its lightweight nature, high rigidity, excellent flatness, and easy fabrication. ACP panels are available in a wide range of colours, textures, and finishes including mirror, matte, metallic, and wood grain — making them one of the most versatile cladding materials available today.

Panel Varieties

Types of ACP Panels We Offer

Standard ACP Panels

Plain solid color panels — ideal for offices and commercial buildings. Cost-effective and widely available in 100+ colors.

Fire Retardant (FR) ACP Panels

PVDF coated, FR core panels — ideal for high-rise buildings, hospitals, and public spaces with strict fire safety requirements.

Mirror Finish ACP Panels

Reflective surface panels — ideal for showrooms, retail facades, and buildings requiring a premium, high-gloss look.

Wooden Finish ACP Panels

Wood texture look panels — ideal for hospitality, restaurants, and residential projects requiring a natural aesthetic.

Metallic Finish ACP Panels

Brushed or anodized metallic look — ideal for corporate office buildings and IT parks requiring a modern industrial aesthetic.

Stone Finish ACP Panels

Marble or granite texture finish — ideal for luxury buildings, banks, and premium commercial projects.

How We Work

Our ACP Cladding Installation Process

01

Site Survey & Measurement

Our team visits your site to take accurate measurements and assess the facade structure for subframe planning.

02

Design & Panel Selection

We help you choose the right ACP panel color, finish, and thickness based on your building type and requirements.

03

Fabrication

Panels are precision-cut and fabricated at our workshop as per the approved design and site dimensions.

04

Framework / Sub-Structure

A galvanized iron or aluminium subframe is installed on the wall to provide a secure support structure for the cladding panels.

05

Panel Installation

ACP panels are fixed securely to the subframe using rivets, screws, or bonding methods with precise alignment.

06

Finishing & Sealant

Joints are sealed with weather-resistant silicone sealant for a clean, watertight, professional finish.

07

Quality Inspection

Final inspection to ensure alignment, finish quality, and structural integrity before project handover.
