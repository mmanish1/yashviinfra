Completed Metal & Wooden False Ceiling Project — Vadodara

Yashvi Infra Co has completed another metal false ceiling and wooden false ceiling installation in Vadodara, combining the durability of metal ceiling systems with the warm, natural look of wooden finishes. Here's a look at the project, the materials used, and why this combination works so well for modern interiors.

Project Overview

This Vadodara project required a ceiling design that could deliver a premium, wooden-textured appearance while retaining the strength, fire resistance, and low-maintenance benefits of a metal false ceiling. Our team used wooden-finish metal panels alongside select wooden battens to achieve a warm, layered ceiling design suited to the space's commercial-residential character.

Why Metal Ceiling with a Wooden Finish?

  • Aesthetic warmth: Wooden-finish metal panels replicate the look of real wood without the maintenance concerns of natural timber.
  • Durability: Galvanized and aluminium cores resist moisture, warping, and termite damage, unlike solid wood ceilings.
  • Fire safety: Metal-based wooden-finish panels offer better fire resistance than traditional wooden ceilings.
  • Low maintenance: No polishing, sealing, or termite treatment required over the ceiling's lifetime.
  • Design flexibility: Available in baffle, linear, and open-cell formats to suit both commercial and residential layouts.

Materials & Specifications Used

The project used wooden-finish aluminium and GI baffle panels for the primary ceiling structure, paired with a T-grid support system for precise alignment. Panel thickness and spacing were selected to balance acoustic performance with the desired wooden-grain visual effect, keeping the installation durable for long-term commercial and residential use.

Installation Process

Our team followed a structured execution process across the Vadodara site:

  1. Site survey and ceiling layout planning
  2. Grid and suspension system installation
  3. Fitting of wooden-finish metal baffle/linear panels
  4. Alignment checks and gap-free finishing
  5. Final quality inspection and client handover

Result

The completed ceiling gives the space a warm, wooden ambience while offering the strength and longevity of a metal false ceiling system — a practical, design-forward solution for clients in Vadodara looking for a wooden look without the upkeep of natural wood.
