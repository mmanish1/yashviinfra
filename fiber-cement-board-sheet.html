FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is fiber cement board?

Fiber cement board is a composite building material made from Portland cement, silica sand, and cellulose fibers. It is fire-resistant, moisture-proof, and termite-resistant — ideal for exterior cladding, false ceilings, wall partitions, and flooring.

What are the uses of fiber cement board?

Fiber cement boards are used for exterior wall cladding, false ceilings, internal wall partitions, wet area linings, floor underlays, and industrial applications — suitable for both commercial and residential construction.

Is fiber cement board fire resistant?

Yes — fiber cement board is non-combustible with a Class A fire rating. It does not burn, spread flames, or emit toxic gases, making it the preferred choice for hospitals, schools, and commercial high-rises.

How long does fiber cement board last?

Quality fiber cement boards last 30–50 years with minimal maintenance. They resist moisture, termites, UV rays, and weathering — one of the most durable building materials available in the market.
