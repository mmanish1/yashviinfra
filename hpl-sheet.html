What Is It?

What is HPL (High Pressure Laminate) Sheet?

HPL (High Pressure Laminate) sheet is a decorative surface material manufactured by bonding multiple layers of kraft paper and a decorative paper under extremely high pressure and temperature. The result is an exceptionally strong, dense, and hard-wearing panel with a beautiful decorative surface. Available in hundreds of colors, textures, and finishes — including solid colors, woodgrain, stone, and abstract patterns — HPL sheets are used for exterior cladding, interior wall panels, furniture surfaces, toilet cubicle partitions, laboratory countertops, and locker panels.

HPL Varieties

Types of HPL Sheets We Supply & Install

Exterior Compact HPL

Thick compact panels (6mm–13mm) designed for outdoor wall cladding and facades. UV-resistant, weatherproof, and highly durable for Gujarat's climate.

Interior Wall Panel HPL

Thinner sheets (3mm–6mm) for interior wall cladding, reception areas, corridors, and commercial interiors. Wide range of colors and finishes.

Toilet Cubicle HPL

Moisture-resistant compact HPL panels for washroom and toilet cubicle partitions. Anti-bacterial surface, easy to clean, ideal for high-traffic restrooms.

Woodgrain Finish HPL

Natural wood texture laminate sheets — ideal for building facades, office interiors, and commercial spaces requiring a warm, natural aesthetic.

Stone & Concrete Finish HPL

Marble, granite, and concrete texture HPL sheets — ideal for premium building facades, lobbies, and reception areas requiring a luxury finish.

Solid Color HPL Panels

Plain solid color compact HPL sheets — ideal for modern, contemporary building exteriors, school facades, hospitals, and institutional buildings.
