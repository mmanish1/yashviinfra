U-Shape P50 Exterior Louver in Ahmedabad & Gujarat

The U-Shape P50 is the most widely installed exterior louver across commercial and residential buildings in Ahmedabad and Gujarat. With blades at a 50mm centre-to-centre pitch, it delivers the ideal balance — meaningful sun shading without the solid, screen-like appearance of a denser pitch, and good natural ventilation without the wide-open look of a larger spacing.

Where P25 leans toward maximum coverage and P75 leans toward a bold open look, P50 works well for almost any project — and that's why it's the default recommendation for most building facades, office screens, and architectural louver installations across Ahmedabad, Surat, Vadodara, Gandhinagar, and all of Gujarat.

The Right Balance for Most Buildings in Ahmedabad

P50 provides meaningful solar control — reducing facade heat gain significantly during Ahmedabad's summer — while keeping the louver visually light and architectural rather than screen-like. It doesn't look too closed or too open on any typical building orientation.

Classic Louver Visual — Works on Any Facade Style

The 50mm blade rhythm is the "standard" louver look that architects, developers, and building committees across Gujarat recognize and approve — clean, professional, and consistent with modern commercial architecture from Ahmedabad to Surat.

Works Across All Building Orientations

Unlike P25 which is specifically suited to high-sun west/south walls, P50 performs acceptably on north, south, east, and west-facing facades — making it the practical choice when a building has multiple louver faces with different sun exposures.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a U-Shape P50 louver?

The U-Shape P50 is an exterior aluminium louver with U-profile vertical blades at a 50mm pitch — blades spaced 50mm centre-to-centre. It is the most popular all-purpose facade louver in Ahmedabad and Gujarat, offering a balanced combination of sun shading, natural ventilation, and classic architectural appearance that suits any building type.

Why is P50 the most popular louver pitch?

P50 hits the middle ground most projects need — enough blade coverage to meaningfully reduce solar heat gain, while keeping the louver open enough to look light and architectural rather than like a solid screen. It works across all building orientations and is the default choice for architects and contractors across Gujarat.

What is the difference between P50 and P25?

P25 has blades 25mm apart — denser, more shading, nearer to a solid privacy screen. P50 has blades 50mm apart — more open, classic louver look, good shading with better transparency and airflow. Choose P25 when maximum screening is the priority; P50 for the standard balanced architectural look.
