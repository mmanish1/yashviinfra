U-Shaped Baffle Ceiling in Ahmedabad & Gujarat

A U-shaped baffle ceiling is a suspended metal ceiling system made of vertical U-profile blades that hang downward from a concealed ceiling structure. Unlike flat panel ceilings, baffle ceilings create a stunning three-dimensional visual effect — with depth, shadow play, and a dramatic architectural presence that transforms any large space.

Yashvi Infra Co supplies and installs U-shaped baffle ceiling systems in aluminum and GI material, available in multiple blade heights (50mm to 200mm), adjustable pitch spacing, and custom powder-coat colors. Widely used in hotel lobbies, shopping mall atriums, corporate reception areas, airports, restaurants, and premium commercial interiors across Ahmedabad, Surat, Vadodara, Gandhinagar, and all of Gujarat.

Dramatic 3D Visual Effect

Vertical blades hanging from the ceiling structure create depth, shadow, and rhythm — making baffle ceilings the most architecturally striking ceiling system available for large commercial spaces.

Full Service & MEP Access

Unlike closed panel ceilings, the open baffle system allows full access to HVAC ducts, fire sprinklers, electrical conduits, and lighting above — ideal for commercial spaces requiring regular service access.

Acoustic Performance

The vertical blade geometry disrupts sound reflection and reduces echo in large open spaces. Optional acoustic infill inside the blade cavity further improves sound absorption — ideal for atriums, lobbies, and conference areas.

How It Works

How U-Shaped Baffle Ceiling Is Installed

1

Site Survey & Ceiling Height Measurement

Our team measures the ceiling height, span, and space dimensions. We recommend the appropriate blade height, pitch spacing, and layout direction based on your architecture and design intent.

2

Suspension System Installation

Hanger rods are anchored to the RCC slab using anchor bolts. A concealed carrier rail system is installed at the required drop height — levelled precisely using a laser level.

3

Baffle Blade Fixing

U-profile blades are hooked or clipped onto the carrier rails at the specified pitch spacing. Each blade hangs vertically downward — aligned perfectly in parallel rows across the ceiling span.

4

Light & Service Integration

LED strip lights, recessed spotlights, and flood lights are integrated into the blade carrier system. Fire sprinkler heads and AC supply grilles are positioned between blade rows as per the service drawing.

5

Final Alignment & Handover

All blades are checked for level, parallel alignment, and equal pitch spacing. The ceiling is inspected and a final walkthrough handover is done with the client before project completion.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a U-shaped baffle ceiling?

A U-shaped baffle ceiling is a suspended metal ceiling made of vertical U-profile blades hanging downward from a concealed carrier system. Unlike flat panel ceilings, it creates a dramatic 3D visual effect with depth and shadow — widely used in hotel lobbies, malls, airports, and commercial atriums across Ahmedabad and Gujarat.

What blade sizes are available for baffle ceiling?

Yashvi Infra Co offers U-shaped baffle blades in 50mm, 75mm, 100mm, 150mm, and 200mm heights — in aluminum or GI, with 0.5mm to 0.8mm thickness. Custom blade heights and mixed-blade designs are available for unique architectural projects.

Do baffle ceilings improve acoustics?

Yes. The vertical blade geometry of a baffle ceiling disrupts sound reflection and reduces echo and reverberation in large open spaces. With optional mineral wool acoustic infill inside the blade cavity, baffle ceilings significantly improve sound absorption — ideal for atriums, conference halls, and open offices.

What is the difference between baffle ceiling and linear ceiling?

A linear ceiling (84C, 84R, 150F) has flat horizontal panels creating a uniform surface. A baffle ceiling has vertical blades hanging downward, creating a 3D architectural effect with visible depth and shadow. Baffle ceilings are chosen for visual impact and acoustic performance; linear ceilings for a clean flat finish.
