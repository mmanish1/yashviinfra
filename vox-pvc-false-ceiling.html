VOX PVC False Ceiling in Ahmedabad & Gujarat

VOX PVC false ceiling is a modern, high-quality ceiling solution made from premium polyvinyl chloride (PVC) panels. Lightweight, 100% waterproof, and available in a wide range of elegant colors and textures, VOX PVC ceilings are the ideal choice for both residential and commercial spaces. Yashvi Infra Co is a trusted VOX PVC false ceiling supplier and installer serving Ahmedabad, Surat, Vadodara, and all of Gujarat.

Our VOX PVC ceiling systems are engineered for durability, easy installation, and long-lasting performance. Unlike gypsum or metal ceilings, PVC panels are completely resistant to moisture, termites, mould, and humidity — making them perfect for bathrooms, kitchens, living rooms, offices, retail spaces, and any area exposed to dampness. With minimal maintenance and a lifespan of 15–25 years, VOX PVC false ceiling is a smart, cost-effective investment.

Why Choose VOX PVC False Ceiling

100% Waterproof

PVC does not absorb moisture. It will never warp, swell, crack, or develop mould — making it the only ceiling material truly suited for bathrooms and kitchens.

Termite & Pest Proof

PVC is completely inorganic — no food source for termites or any pest. Zero chemical treatment required, saving ongoing pest control costs.

Unlimited Design Options

Plain white, wood grain, marble, geometric, 3D texture — hundreds of design options so your ceiling perfectly complements your interior theme.

Lightweight & Easy to Install

PVC panels are significantly lighter than gypsum or metal — reducing structural load, cutting installation time, and lowering total project cost.

Zero Maintenance

Just wipe with a damp cloth. No painting, no polishing, no resealing ever needed. VOX PVC ceilings look as fresh as day one for years.

Cost-Effective Investment

Lower material cost than metal and lower lifetime maintenance cost than gypsum — PVC false ceiling delivers the best value for both budget and premium projects.
