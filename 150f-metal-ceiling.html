Linear 150F Metal Ceiling for Petrol Pump & CNG Pump in Ahmedabad

The Linear 150F is a flat-edge wide metal ceiling panel with a 150mm face width — the largest standard linear ceiling profile we offer. The "F" stands for Flat, meaning the panel edge is completely flat with no curve or C-shape, giving a clean, bold, uninterrupted ceiling plane. It is the most popular metal ceiling choice for petrol pump canopies, CNG pump stations, and fuel station forecourts across Ahmedabad and Gujarat.

Yashvi Infra Co supplies and installs Linear 150F ceiling systems in aluminum and GI material — weatherproof, non-combustible, UV-resistant, and available in standard white and custom RAL colors to match fuel company brand guidelines (HPCL, BPCL, Indian Oil, Reliance BP, Shell, and others). We serve petrol pump owners, CNG station contractors, and commercial builders across Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Surat, Vadodara, and all of Gujarat.

Petrol Pump & CNG

Why 150F Is the Standard Ceiling for Petrol Pumps & CNG Stations in Gujarat

Fuel station canopies face conditions that no ordinary ceiling can handle — outdoor exposure, heat, fuel vapour, rain, and strict fire safety regulations. The Linear 150F is purpose-built for this environment.

Non-Combustible

Metal is inherently non-combustible — Class A fire rated. Essential for petrol pump and CNG station canopies where fire safety regulations are strictly enforced by PESO (Petroleum and Explosives Safety Organisation).

Weatherproof

Powder-coated aluminum and GI panels resist Gujarat's heavy monsoon rain, UV radiation, and extreme summer heat — maintaining their color and structural integrity for 15–25 years without repainting.

Brand Color Matching

Every major oil company (HPCL, BPCL, Indian Oil, Reliance BP, Shell, Essar) has specific color standards. We match the exact RAL or Pantone specification required for your fuel company franchise.

Ventilation-Friendly

Adjustable gap between 150F panels allows natural air circulation under the — essential for CNG pump stations where gas ventilation is required and for reducing heat build-up in open forecourt areas.

LED Lighting Integration

LED strip lights, recessed downlights, and flood lamps integrate cleanly within the 150F panel system — fully cuttable on site to accommodate any lighting layout required by your fuel company specifications.

Easy to Clean

Petrol pump ceilings accumulate dust, fuel residue, and bird droppings. The smooth powder-coated surface of 150F panels cleans easily with water — requiring no special maintenance over the years.

150F vs 84C / 84R — Which to Choose?

F

Choose 150F if…

Your space is a petrol pump , CNG station, mall corridor, airport, large showroom, or any wide open area — where wider panels and fewer joints give a bolder, cleaner ceiling plane. Also ideal when a flat profile is required by franchise specifications.

C

Choose 84C / 184C if…

Your space is a medium-sized office, hospital corridor, classroom, or retail counter where a finer linear look is preferred, or where a wooden grain finish is required.

R

Choose 84R / 184R if…

Your space is a hotel, premium office, or upscale retail area where a soft round-edge shadow line creates a more refined, elegant interior finish.
