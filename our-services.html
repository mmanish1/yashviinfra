HPL Sheet (High-Pressure Laminate)

High-Pressure Laminate (HPL) sheets are highly durable decorative surface materials used for wall cladding, furniture, partitions, countertops, and facade applications. Known for exceptional scratch resistance, impact resistance, and moisture performance, HPL is the preferred surface material for hospitals, schools, offices, laboratories, and commercial interiors across Ahmedabad and Gujarat.

  • Exterior Facade & Wall Cladding
  • Interior Wall Panelling & Partitions
  • Countertops & Workstation Surfaces
  • Toilet Cubicle Partition Panels
  • Locker & Furniture Surfaces
  • Compact, Standard & Postforming Grades
Explore HPL Sheets
Service 06

Fiber Cement Board & Sheet

Fiber cement board is a composite building material made from Portland cement, silica sand, and cellulose fibers — delivering Class A fire resistance, 100% moisture protection, and termite-proof performance. Ideal for exterior wall cladding, false ceilings, internal partitions, wet area linings, and industrial applications. With a 30–50 year lifespan, it is one of the most durable and cost-effective building materials available across Ahmedabad and Gujarat.

  • Flat Fiber Cement Sheets
  • Textured & Wood-Grain Finish Sheets
  • Compressed High-Density Boards
  • Perforated Acoustic Sheets
  • Large-Format Cladding Panels
  • False Ceiling & Soffit Boards
Explore Fiber Cement
