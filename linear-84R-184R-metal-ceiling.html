Linear 84R / 184R Metal Ceiling in Ahmedabad & Gujarat

The Linear 84R and 184R are round-edge GI metal false ceiling panels — designed for spaces that demand a softer, more refined visual finish compared to the sharp C-edge of the 84C profile. The rounded edge creates a gentle shadow line between panels, adding depth and elegance to any ceiling in Ahmedabad and Gujarat.

Yashvi Infra Co supplies and installs 84R and 184R panels in GI (Galvanised Iron) material, available in 0.5mm and 0.6mm thickness, with panel lengths up to 5,000mm (5 meters). Finished in powder-coat white, off-white, or custom RAL colors — these panels are a popular choice for hotels, hospitality spaces, premium offices, hospitals, and commercial interiors across Ahmedabad, Surat, Vadodara, and all of Gujarat.

Simple rule of thumb

Choose 84R / 184R if…

Your space is a hotel lobby, restaurant, premium office reception, or high-end retail store — anywhere a softer, more luxurious ceiling finish is preferred over a sharp industrial look.

Choose 84C / 184C if…

Your space is an IT office, hospital, school, or industrial facility — where a crisp, clean sharp-edge finish is preferred and wooden grain finish or aluminum material is required.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Linear 84R metal ceiling?

Linear 84R is a round-edge GI metal false ceiling panel with an 84mm face width. The rounded edge creates a soft shadow line between panels for a more elegant finish. It is available in 0.5mm and 0.6mm thickness with panel lengths up to 5,000mm (5 meters).

What is the maximum length of 84R / 184R panels?

The maximum panel length for Linear 84R and 184R ceiling panels supplied by Yashvi Infra Co is 5,000mm (5 meters). Custom lengths up to 5 meters are available on order. Panels longer than 5 meters are not available in the R-profile range.

What thickness is available for 84R / 184R panels?

Yashvi Infra Co supplies 84R and 184R panels in GI (Galvanised Iron) only, in two thickness options — 0.5mm for standard applications and 0.6mm for greater rigidity on longer spans and high-traffic spaces.

What is the difference between 84R and 84C ceiling panels?

The 84R has a round edge — creating a softer, more refined shadow line — while the 84C has a sharp C-edge for a crisper, more commercial look. Both are 84mm wide. The 84R is GI only; the 84C is available in both aluminum and GI with wooden finish options.

Does Yashvi Infra Co install 84R / 184R ceiling in Ahmedabad?

Yes — we supply and install Linear 84R and 184R round-edge metal false ceilings across Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Surat, Vadodara, and all of Gujarat. Free site visit, itemised quote, in-house installation team, and warranty support included.
