Open Cell Ceiling in Ahmedabad & Gujarat

An open cell ceiling is a metal grid ceiling system where interlocking aluminum blades form a square or rectangular cell pattern — leaving the cells fully open so services, ducts, and the structural slab above remain visible. It delivers a clean, modern industrial aesthetic while providing full unrestricted access to MEP services, fire sprinklers, HVAC ducts, and electrical conduits.

Yashvi Infra Co supplies and installs open cell ceiling systems in aluminum and GI material, available in multiple cell sizes from 50×50mm to 200×200mm, with custom blade heights and powder-coat finishes. The most popular ceiling choice for car parks, basement lobbies, malls, airports, industrial facilities, and commercial atriums across Ahmedabad, Surat, Vadodara, and all of Gujarat.

Car Park Ceiling Specialist

Why Open Cell Ceiling Is the #1 Choice for Car Parks in Ahmedabad

Car park ceilings have unique requirements that most ceilings can't meet. Open cell is specifically designed for this environment — and here's why it's installed in nearly every multi-level car park across Gujarat.

Fire Sprinkler Compliance

Open cells allow smoke to rise freely to sprinkler heads and smoke detectors above — meeting NFPA and NBC fire safety code requirements for enclosed car parks without a complex separate sprinkler drop system.

Natural Ventilation

Car exhaust fumes and CO buildup are a safety hazard in enclosed car parks. The open grid allows HVAC fresh air to circulate freely through the ceiling — reducing pollutant concentrations and meeting ventilation requirements.

Full Maintenance Access

Electrical conduits, CCTV cameras, PA systems, and emergency lighting above the ceiling remain fully visible and accessible — no panels to remove for maintenance or emergency access in car parks across Ahmedabad.

Lighting Integration

LED strip lights and flood lamps mount directly to the carrier system above the open cell grid — illuminating the grid beautifully from above while keeping fittings protected and easily replaceable.

Transforms Bare Concrete

Without open cell ceiling, car park ceilings are bare exposed concrete with visible MEP. Open cell transforms this into a clean, finished space — raising the perceived quality of the entire building for visitors and residents.

Cost-Effective vs Closed Ceiling

Open cell is significantly more cost-effective than a closed linear or gypsum ceiling for car parks — no additional fire sprinkler drops, no AC cut-outs needed, and faster installation reduces total project cost.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an open cell ceiling?

An open cell ceiling is a metal grid ceiling system where interlocking aluminum or GI blades form a square or rectangular cell pattern — with the cells left fully open so services above remain visible and accessible. It delivers a modern industrial look with full MEP access, fire sprinkler compliance, and natural ventilation.

What cell sizes are available?

Yashvi Infra Co offers open cell ceiling in 50×50mm, 75×75mm, 100×100mm, 150×150mm, and 200×200mm cell sizes — in aluminum or GI. The most popular size across car parks and commercial spaces in Ahmedabad and Gujarat is 100×100mm. Custom rectangular sizes are available on order.

Is open cell ceiling suitable for car parks?

Yes — open cell ceiling is the #1 choice for car park ceilings across Gujarat. The open grid allows smoke to rise to sprinkler heads, HVAC air to circulate freely, and all services to remain accessible — meeting fire safety and ventilation requirements without additional infrastructure.

What is the difference between open cell and baffle ceiling?

Open cell has a flat horizontal grid viewed from below — clean and geometric. Baffle ceiling has vertical blades hanging downward — dramatic 3D depth effect. Open cell is chosen for car parks, basements, and industrial spaces; baffle is chosen for lobbies, atriums, and premium commercial interiors.
