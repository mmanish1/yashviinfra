Not sure which to choose?

Here is a simple rule used by our designers for ceiling projects in Ahmedabad:

1

Choose 84C if your space is a small office cabin, corridor, bedroom, or retail counter area — the narrower panels make the room feel longer and more precise.

2

Choose 184C if your space is a large open office, mall corridor, showroom, or restaurant — the wider panels fill the ceiling more boldly with fewer visible joints.

3

Mix both — some of the most striking false ceiling designs in Ahmedabad combine 84C and 184C panels in the same space for a dynamic, tiered visual effect.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Linear 84C metal ceiling?

Linear 84C is a C-shaped metal false ceiling panel with 84mm face width. It is available in aluminum or GI with plain white, wooden grain, and custom powder-coat finishes. Widely installed for false ceiling in Ahmedabad across offices, malls, hospitals, and homes.

Is wooden finish available for ceiling in Ahmedabad?

Yes. Yashvi Infra Co supplies and installs 84C and 184C panels in realistic wooden ceiling finish in Ahmedabad — teak, oak, walnut, and wenge options available. The wood grain powder coat gives the look of real timber with zero maintenance.

What is the difference between 84C and 184C panels?

The 84C has an 84mm face width — a finer, more detailed linear look suited for small to medium spaces. The 184C has a 184mm width — bolder, wider panels best for large open areas like malls and showrooms. Both use the same C-profile and suspension system.
