U-Shape P75 Exterior Louver in Ahmedabad & Gujarat

The U-Shape P75 is the most open of the standard U-shape louver range — vertical aluminium blades at a 75mm centre-to-centre pitch. With wider gaps between blades, P75 reads as bold and architectural rather than screen-like, making it the choice when the facade calls for a strong visual rhythm without feeling closed or heavy.

P75 is chosen when ventilation, visual openness, and architectural character are the priority — for feature facades, open-air staircase screens, decorative cladding, and north or east-facing walls in Ahmedabad and Gujarat where direct solar shading is not the main requirement. Supplied in aluminium and GI with any powder-coat color.

Maximum Ventilation — Most Open Pitch

75mm between blades allows the most free air movement of any standard U-shape pitch — ideal for spaces where heat dissipation and natural cross-ventilation through the louver is the primary functional requirement.

Bold Blade Rhythm — Strong Architectural Character

At 75mm spacing, each individual blade is clearly visible as a design element — creating a strong, rhythmic architectural pattern on the facade that draws the eye without overwhelming it. A favourite of architects designing modern commercial buildings in Ahmedabad.

Lighter, Less Material — More Cost-Effective

Fewer blades per metre of facade means less material cost and faster installation compared to P25 or P50 — making P75 the most economical U-shape louver option when the project budget is a factor and full shading coverage is not required.

When to Choose P75

Is P75 the Right Choice for Your Project?

Choose P75 when…

Your wall faces north or east in Ahmedabad — lower direct sun means less shading is needed and the open look of P75 works well. Also ideal when the louver is primarily decorative — a feature element on the facade rather than a functional sun blocker. And when maximum ventilation through the louver is a functional requirement: equipment enclosures, open-air corridors, parking structures, and ventilated cladding systems.

Consider P50 or P25 instead when…

Your wall faces west or south and takes 4+ hours of direct afternoon sun — P75's wider gaps let more solar radiation through than most west-facing facades in Ahmedabad's summer can afford. For maximum shade, choose P25; for balanced shade and look, choose P50.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a U-Shape P75 louver?

The U-Shape P75 is an exterior aluminium louver with U-profile vertical blades at a 75mm centre-to-centre pitch — the most open of the standard U-shape range. It delivers a bold architectural look with maximum natural ventilation, and is chosen for decorative facades, car park screens, open-air corridors, and north/east-facing building walls in Ahmedabad and Gujarat.

When should I choose P75 over P50 or P25?

Choose P75 when the louver is primarily architectural or decorative, when the wall faces north or east with minimal direct sun, or when maximum airflow through the louver is the main functional requirement. For walls facing west or south with heavy sun exposure, P50 or P25 will provide more effective solar shading.

Does P75 provide sun shading?

P75 provides moderate sun shading — less than P25 or P50 due to the wider 75mm gap between blades. It reduces some direct solar radiation but is not suited for maximum sun control on west or south-facing facades in Ahmedabad's intense summer climate. For high-shading applications, choose P25 or P50.
